Frequently asked

Good questions.
Straight answers.

It sounds like you want to make sure this is worth your time before you commit. That's exactly the right instinct.

What exactly is a NAP audit?
A NAP audit checks whether your business Name, Address, and Phone number appear identically across every major online directory. AI tools like ChatGPT, Google's AI Overview, and Siri all cross-reference these listings before recommending local businesses. Inconsistencies signal unreliability and get you deprioritized.
Why don't you fix the listings for me?
Many directories require the business owner to log in and verify their identity before making changes. We can't do that for you — and honestly, fixing 10 listings takes about 30 minutes with our report in hand. You or your assistant can handle it the same afternoon you receive it.
How is this different from a free tool?
Free tools give you a score. We give you a line-by-line breakdown of exactly what each directory says versus what your GBP says, with a direct link to update each one. There's no interpretation needed — it's a checklist your team can complete immediately.
How long until I see results?
Once you fix your listings, most directories re-crawl within 2–6 weeks. AI tools typically update their local data within a similar window. Some businesses see improvement in AI recommendations within 30–60 days of fixing their NAP consistency.
What if I only have one or two inconsistencies?
Even one inconsistency can suppress your visibility if it's on a high-authority directory. More importantly, most businesses we audit have far more issues than they expect — often from listings created years ago that were never updated after a move or phone change.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes. Payment is handled entirely by Stripe — one of the most trusted payment processors in the world. Your card information never touches our servers. We only receive a notification that payment was completed.
My business already has an SEO agency. Why do I need this?
Most agencies focus on traditional search. AI recommendation systems — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google’s AI Overviews — decide who to recommend based on different signals: NAP consistency across 30+ directories, schema accuracy, GBP completeness, and local authority. Very few agencies audit these systematically, and even fewer understand how upstream data providers like Data Axle silently corrupt your listings. This audit covers what your current agency almost certainly isn’t checking.
What's the Content Authority Analysis and do I need it?
The audit tells AI tools that your business is legitimate and trustworthy. The Content Authority Analysis tells them what you do — mapping your GBP categories and services to a complete page architecture with written titles, meta descriptions, and content outlines for each service page. It's for businesses that want a complete AI-ready content strategy, not just the technical foundation. Most businesses benefit from the audit first and then decide. It's never required to get value from the core report.
Do I need to know anything about SEO or technical stuff to use this?
No. The report is written for business owners, not developers. Every issue is explained in plain language with a direct link to the listing that needs updating. If you can log into Yelp and edit your phone number, you can fix everything in this report.
